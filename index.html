Hi there, I'm Lei Yang!

I received my B.Sc (2011.07) and M.Sc degrees (supervisor: Prof. Zheng-Hai Huang, 2014.07) in Applied Mathematics at Tianjin University. I started my PhD study in 2014.08 in Department of Applied Mathematics at Hong Kong Polytechnic University under supervision of my Chief-supervisor: Prof. Xiaojun Chen and my Co-supervisor: Prof. Ting Kei Pong. I got my Ph.D. degree in 2017.09. I was a Postdoctoral Fellow from 2017.10 to 2017.12 in Department of Applied Mathematics at Hong Kong Polytechnic University hosted by Prof. Xiaojun Chen and was a Research Fellow from 2018.01 to 2020.07 in Institute of Operations Research and Analytics at National University of Singapore hosted by Prof. Kim-Chuan Toh. I am now a Research Fellow in Department of Mathematics at National University of Singapore hosted by Prof. Kim-Chuan Toh.
